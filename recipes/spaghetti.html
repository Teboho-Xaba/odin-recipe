Spaghetti

Roll Up!!!


Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • A jar of marinara sause
  • 1-2 pounds of ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Pots and utensils
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions for dish

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Salt
  3. Add the Pasta and stir
  4. Taste
  5. Drain
  6. Remove Ravioli
  7. Add pasta water
  8. Bon Apetit
