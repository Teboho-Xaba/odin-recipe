Pasta Meal

Delectable Sight, innit?

The cheese sauce is oh so rich, thick, silky, and creamy, the pasta is al dente, and the breadcrumb topping is crisp and crunch


Ingredients
  • Cheese
  • Macaroni
  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Flour
  • Salt
Instructions for preparation
  1. 6 cups water
  2. 4 tbsp. margarine or butter
  3. 1/4 cup 2% milk
  4. Boil water in medium saucepan
  5. 9:00 stir in macaroni
  6. Cook 8 to 10 min. or until tender, stirring occasionally.
  7. Drain: Do not rinse. Return to pan
  8. Add margarine, milk and cheese sauce mix; mix well.
  9. To prepare with less fat: Prepare as directed, using 2 tsp. unsalted butter and 1/4 cup fat-free milk
