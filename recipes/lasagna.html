Lasagna Dish

How about them layers?

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 large bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 (28-ounce) can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 crushed tomatoe
  • 2 tablespoons chopped oregano
  • 1/4 parsely
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • 1/4 Parmesan cheese
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese

Instructions to prepare me

  1. Put pasta in water to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook and add the pepper, onions and garlic to the beef
  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Heat the oven to 375Â°F
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
